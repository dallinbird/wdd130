Total Cost:

Cost of the car:
This is the price tag of the car that you wish to purchase.
DownPayment:
Any upfront cost that needs to be paid for out of pocket and can't be covered by a loan.
Years to pay off loan:
How long it will take to pay off the loan. see interest rate for more details but feel free to play around with this one so see how it affects the overall interest payed and the total price.
Interest rate:
This will be the interest rate as applied to your loan. If you do not have a loan alrady lined up feel free to play around with different rates and see what affects you. typicaly the Higher the rate the lower the time to pay off the loan, whereas the lower the rate the longer the loan term will be in time.
Registration:
Each state is different in registration prices. Call up you local dmv and they should be able to give you a price. this can also be different depending on what type of car you are purchasing
Taxes:
Each state is different in their tax rates for buying new or used cars. Call up you local dmv and see if they can give a applicable tax rate. This rate will be applied to the overall cost of the car purchased.
Dealership fees:
This is a tricky one because it is dependent on the actual dealer of the car. I recomend that you ask the dealer what the cost will be and see if they will tell you.
Other costs:
Any other costs that are not covered by any of the previous inputs can be put here.
